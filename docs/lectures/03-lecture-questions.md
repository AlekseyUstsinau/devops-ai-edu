### Question 1

A) Multi-Cloud Platform  
B) Model Context Protocol  
C) Master Control Program  
D) Managed Copilot Provider  

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 2

A) To train new AI models  
B) To provide standardized way for AI systems to access external data and tools
